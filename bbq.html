🔥 How to Grill Beef

1. Wait until the grill gets hot and everything starts to sizzle. 🔥🔥🔥

2. Once one side of the meat turns brown, flip it once and lower the heat. 🔥🔥

3. When all sides are nicely browned, reduce the heat to low and enjoy!

4. You can turn off the heat while eating — just let us know, and we'll turn it back on when you're ready!

🔥 How to Grill Pork

1. Wait until the grill is hot enough before placing the pork on.

2. Don’t flip too frequently — let each side cook until it becomes crispy and golden brown.

3. Try grilling some garlic and onion together for extra flavor!
