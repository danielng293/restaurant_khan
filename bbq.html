🔥 How to Grill Beef Barbeque

1. Place the beef in the center and the noodles/mushrooms around the edges. Wait until one side of the meat turns deliciously brown. (Heat:🔥🔥🔥)

2. Once one side turns brown, flip the meat just once and slightly lower the heat. (Heat:🔥🔥)

3. When all sides are nicely browned, reduce the heat to the lowest setting and enjoy! (Heat:🔥)

4. Move the cooked meat to the sides of the grill so it can soak up the sauce. You can turn off the heat while eating — just let us know, and we’ll turn it back on for you!

🔥 How to Grill Pork

1. Wait until the grill is hot enough before placing the pork on.

2. Don’t flip too frequently — let each side cook until it becomes crispy and golden brown.

3. Try grilling some garlic and onion together for extra flavor!
