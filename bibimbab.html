🍚 How to Eat Bibimbap (비빔밥)

  1. Before you mix it, take a photo — it’s beautiful! 📸
  2. Add some gochujang (고추장, Korean chili paste), and mix it well with your spoon! Adjust the amount based on your spice level.
  3. Take a bite first, and then add more gochujang if you like it spicier!
