🌿 How to Make a Wrap(쌈) with Bulgogi or BBQ

🥬 Basic Wrap 기본 쌈

1. Take a fresh piece of lettuce from your plate.

2. Add a slice of bulgogi or barbequed meat.

3. Top it off with a small scoop of ssamjang (쌈장, Korean wrapping sauce).

4. Wrap it up and enjoy! Try eating it in one bite — it’s the Korean way (and it keeps your hands clean!).

🔥 Advanced Wrap

1. Start with a lettuce leaf as your base.

2. Add your choice of meat — bulgogi or BBQ.

3. A little ssamjang goes a long way — add just a dab.

4. Level it up: add garlic, chili pepper, or even a small scoop of rice. Be creative!
